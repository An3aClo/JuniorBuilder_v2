Volcano Eruption

You will need:

  • Creative bricks
  • Large base plate
  • Volcano bricks - can build your own depending on the size of your bottle
  • 500ml bottle with top cut off or something similar
  • Old towel to place volcano on
  • Cloth to clean after experiment

Volcano ingredients (per lesson):

  • Vinegar
  • Water
  • Sunlight soap
  • Red food colouring
  • Baking soda

Volcano eruption:

  • Add water, vinegar, soap, colouring to bottle
  • Wrap baking soda in a tissue and drop into bottle when you are ready for it to erupt (pre-make tissue wrap per school)

Instructions:

Give each child a few bricks of the volcano. When it's their turn they can put all their bricks onto the volcano.

Instructor to place bottle with ingredients in the middle, let them watch what you put in the bottle.

Put bottle in middle of the volcano you are building and let them finish building volcano around it.

All children to stand/ sit around the volcano, do a count down from 10 and instructor to drop baking soda ball in bottle and watch the volcano erupt.
